Project Manager

Onsite Full-time
Apply for this Position
Department Project Management
Location Main Office & Project Sites
Experience 5+ Years
Salary Range Competitive

We are seeking an experienced Project Manager to oversee construction projects from inception to completion. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of successfully managing commercial construction projects, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and to our high-quality standards.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain project schedules, budgets, and resources
  • Coordinate with subcontractors, suppliers, and internal teams
  • Conduct regular site visits to monitor progress and ensure quality standards
  • Manage project documentation, permits, and regulatory compliance
  • Identify and resolve project issues and risks
  • Communicate regularly with clients to provide updates and address concerns
  • Lead project meetings and maintain detailed records
  • Ensure adherence to safety protocols and company policies

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Engineering, or related field
  • 5+ years of experience in construction project management
  • Strong knowledge of construction methods, materials, and industry standards
  • Proficiency in project management software and Microsoft Office Suite
  • Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to read and interpret construction drawings and specifications
  • Valid driver's license and ability to travel to project sites
  • PMP certification preferred

Construction Superintendent

Onsite Full-time
Apply for this Position
Department Field Operations
Location Various Project Sites
Experience 7+ Years
Salary Range Competitive

We are looking for an experienced Construction Superintendent to oversee daily construction operations on project sites. The successful candidate will be responsible for coordinating crews, ensuring safety standards, maintaining quality control, and keeping projects on schedule.

Responsibilities

  • Supervise construction activities and coordinate subcontractors on site
  • Implement and enforce safety protocols and quality control measures
  • Review and interpret project plans, specifications, and shop drawings
  • Coordinate with Project Managers to ensure project goals are met
  • Maintain daily logs, progress reports, and other site documentation
  • Address and resolve field issues and construction challenges
  • Monitor material deliveries and manage on-site inventory
  • Conduct regular inspections to ensure compliance with plans and codes

Requirements

  • 7+ years of experience as a Construction Superintendent
  • Strong knowledge of construction methods, building codes, and industry standards
  • Experience with scheduling, cost control, and quality management
  • Excellent leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to read and interpret construction drawings and specifications
  • OSHA training and certification
  • Valid driver's license and ability to work at various project sites
  • Technical or vocational training in construction preferred

Estimator

Office/Remote Full-time
Apply for this Position
Department Preconstruction
Location Main Office (Remote Option Available)
Experience 3+ Years
Salary Range Competitive

We are seeking a detail-oriented Estimator to prepare accurate cost estimates for construction projects. The ideal candidate will have experience analyzing project requirements, soliciting subcontractor quotes, and preparing comprehensive proposals for clients.

Responsibilities

  • Review plans, specifications, and other bid documents to develop accurate cost estimates
  • Perform quantity takeoffs and determine material, labor, and equipment costs
  • Solicit and review subcontractor and supplier quotes
  • Analyze and compare bids to identify the most cost-effective options
  • Prepare detailed cost estimates and bid proposals
  • Collaborate with Project Managers and operations teams
  • Maintain database of current pricing information and historical data
  • Assist with value engineering to optimize costs while maintaining quality

Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience in construction estimating
  • Proficiency in estimating software and Microsoft Excel
  • Strong understanding of construction methods, materials, and costs
  • Ability to read and interpret construction drawings and specifications
  • Excellent analytical, mathematical, and organizational skills
  • Detail-oriented with strong attention to accuracy
  • Effective communication skills for interacting with team members and subcontractors
  • Bachelor's degree in Construction Management or related field preferred
